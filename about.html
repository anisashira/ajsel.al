ABOUT AJSEL HOTEL

Ajsel Hotel in Durrës offers family rooms with air-conditioning, private bathrooms, and balconies. Each room includes a work desk, TV, and soundproofing for a pleasant stay. Guests enjoy a private beach area, rooftop swimming pool, and lush garden.

The family-friendly restaurant offers a buffet breakfast, Italian, and halal options. Additional meal types include vegetarian and gluten-free, catering to diverse dietary needs. Located 34 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Ajsel Hotel is a 2-minute walk from Durres Beach. Nearby attractions include Rock of Kavaje (7 km) and Durres Amphiteatre (4 km). The terrace provides a relaxing outdoor space, while the restaurant serves Greek and Italian cuisine in a modern ambience. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.
