Welcome to Hotel Ajsel
Let your summer story start here


Ajsel Hotel in Durrës offers family rooms with air-conditioning, private bathrooms, and balconies. Each room includes a work desk, TV, and soundproofing for a pleasant stay. Guests enjoy a private beach area, rooftop swimming pool, and lush garden. The terrace provides a relaxing outdoor space, while the restaurant serves Greek and Italian cuisine in a modern ambience. Free WiFi is available throughout the property. The family-friendly restaurant offers a buffet breakfast, Italian, and halal options. Additional meal types include vegetarian and gluten-free, catering to diverse dietary needs. Located 34 km from Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, Ajsel Hotel is a 2-minute walk from Durres Beach. Nearby attractions include Rock of Kavaje (7 km) and Durres Amphiteatre (4 km).

RESTAURANT

Our buffet restaurant is exclusively available for guests with meals included in their stay. It offers a rich selection of fresh and high-quality dishes, including Italian, Mediterranean, and diet-friendly options. The modern and comfortable setting ensures a pleasant dining experience.

BREAKFAST

Ajsel Hotel offers a rich and diverse breakfast buffet, ensuring a great start to the day. Guests can choose from a variety of Italian, halal, vegetarian, and gluten-free options, catering to different dietary needs. The breakfast includes freshly baked pastries, seasonal fruits, dairy products, cereals, and hot dishes, all served in a welcoming and modern setting. With high-quality ingredients and excellent service, the hotel ensures a delicious and satisfying morning experience.
